McDonald v. City of Chicago
Citation: 561 U.S. 742 (2010)

Held: The Fourteenth Amendment incorporates the Second Amendment right, recognized in District of Columbia v. Heller, to keep and bear arms for the purpose of self-defense. The right is fully applicable to the States. Self-defense is a basic right, and the individual right to keep and bear arms is deeply rooted in this Nation's history and tradition.
